Ercüment Orkut is a qualified composer and jazz pianist from Istanbul, Türkiye. His playing style is elegant, powerful, surprising, and melodic. He has been performing around Türkiye and Europe with well known musicians from Dee Dee Bridgewater and China Moses to Kenan Doğulu, Sezen Aksu, Marius Neset, İmer Demirer.

Orkut released 2 solo and 2 band albums since 2015 (Low Profile, Persona, Töz I, Töz II) and is participating in prominent TV series, album recordings and concerts actively as a composer, arranger, and performer.

Coming from a musical family with her well-known conductor father Ender Sakpınar, and jazz musician uncle Ilhan Ersahin, the Swedish-Turkish musician Dilara Sakpınar started music at a very young age. Although she has taken part in many recordings of Ilhan Ersahin and other well-known musicians, she has started her music career with the band 123, with whom she recorded four albums, and played many concerts in Türkiye and around the world.

Continuing her solo career under the alias Lara Di Lara, she offers different experiences to her listeners with her independently released singles, EPs, and new album "Eskisi Gibi Değil" that has just been released.
